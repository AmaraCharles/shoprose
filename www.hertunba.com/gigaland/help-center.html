FAQs

What is OpulaSphere?

A: OpulaSphere is a platform that connects artists, creators and collectors by integrating physical artworks, digital arts and other luxury items with blockchain technology.
What are NFTs and how do they work on OpulaSphere?

A:NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens) are unique digital assets that represent ownership of specific items or content. On OpulaSphere, sellers are helped to create and list NFTs as part of their collections. Buyers can purchase these NFTs, and sellers earn among other benefits.
How do I join OpulaSphere?

A: Register on our website with your details. Once your account is set up, you can start showcasing your art.
How does the Creating Process work on Opulasphere?

A: Artists can mint their artworks by uploading them on the platform, after which they list the artworks. To list each artwork on the platform, there is a listing fee of 0.005 Ethereum per artwork. Once listed, the artwork is available for purchase by our global community.
How do transactions work?

A: Buyers purchase items using Ethereum, and payments are transferred to your digital wallet, ensuring a seamless and secure process.
What are the benefits of using OpulaSphere?

By listing your artwork, you gain exposure to a global audience. We help protect your intellectual property rights through blockchain verification. Transactions are secure, and we offer free shipping for physical items, enhancing the seller and buyer's experience.

What types of artworks can I list on OpulaSphere?

A: You can list a variety of artworks including paintings, sculptures, digital art, jewelry, accessories, and more. Both physical and digital artworks are welcome on our platform.
How does OpulaSphere ensure the security of transactions?

A:OpulaSphere uses blockchain technology to secure all transactions on the platform. This ensures transparency, immutability, and trust in the marketplace. Additionally, we use advanced encryption methods to protect user data and financial information.
What should I do if I encounter an issue on the platform?

A: If you experience any issues or have questions, our live chat support is here to help and assist or you can visit the contact page on our website and fill out the support form, or email us directly at support@shoperosecc.com. We strive to respond to all inquiries as soon as possible since we’re available to assist you any time or day.
How can I stay updated with the latest news and updates from OpulaSphere?

A:Follow us on social media X (formerly Twitter) and subscribe to our newsletter for the latest updates, news, and exclusive offers. You can also check our blog for detailed articles and announcements.
